# Sample Policy

The rebuilt balanced pilot was assembled from:
- AIDev agent-attributed pull request samples
- matched human-control samples from public repositories

This research artifact does not redistribute the underlying raw code corpus. Instead it publishes:
- per-sample metadata
- source provenance
- language labels
- line counts
- content SHA-256 hashes
- aggregate scan results

Rationale:
- preserve reproducibility at the metadata/result level
- avoid casual republication of third-party source files
- keep the public artifact aligned with the aggregate-only research posture used throughout AIRA

Researchers who wish to fully reconstruct the corpus should do so from the original sources under their respective terms and licenses.
